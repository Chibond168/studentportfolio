About Me

Someone dropped me off in Iowa and forgot to pick me up. Because of this, I was stuck in Iowa over 30 years. During those years, in order to make myself useful, I attended college and graduated, I found my wife, and the kids are all grown and on their own. Jobs were fine, at least they paid me good so I could buy a house.

I like to travel from place to place. The scenery, the food, and the local culture open my eyes.

Finally, a tornado went thru Iowa, picked me up and dropped me off in Texas. I am happy in Texas.
